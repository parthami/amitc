Thank you for taking the time for visiting this website. I hope you find the information useful to you and are able to understand your knee problems and the varied treatment strategies available for the best treatment. It may also help you take an informed decision about the treatment you may need under my care.

Any treatment is always as a result of the team effort. The team consists of you as the patient and the medical team in the hospital and primary care. This includes all the ancillary staff which provide the treatment and work hard to make your journey safe and to ensure a good result with and without surgical treatment.

It is therefore important that you take part in the decision making throughout this process.
